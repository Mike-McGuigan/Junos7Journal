# Project: Juno's 7 Mediterranean Journal

## Purpose

To preserve Cameron and Sophie's first season working together aboard **Juno's 7** as a beautiful, lasting family journal.

This project is not primarily a yacht tracker.

It is a personal record of a summer: the places visited, the work done, the messages sent home, the photographs, the short videos, and the small moments that might otherwise be forgotten.

## Guiding Principle

> Don't document the yacht. Document the memories.

## Outputs

The same source material will eventually produce:

- Interactive web journal
- Printable Collector's Edition
- PDF export
- Original media archive
- Route and AIS timeline

## Tone

Warm, elegant, factual and personal.

Preserve Cameron and Sophie's original words exactly.

Do not invent private events.

Where something is inferred from AIS, photographs or context, make that clear.
