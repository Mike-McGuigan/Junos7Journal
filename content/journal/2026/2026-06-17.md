# The Beginning

Cameron and Sophie stepped aboard **Juno's 7** to begin their first season working together on the same yacht.

A marina webcam captured the moment they boarded for the first time. The footage is grainy, but that is part of its value: it preserves the start of the story exactly as it happened.
