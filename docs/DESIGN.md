# Design Notes

## Visual Direction

A premium digital travel journal rather than a traditional website.

Influences:
- National Geographic travel essays
- Boat International
- Apple-style product photography
- Coffee-table books

## Palette

- Deep navy
- Warm ivory
- Sea blue
- Muted gold
- Stone grey

## Typography

- Elegant serif for headings
- Clean sans-serif for navigation and data
- Pull quotes for Cameron and Sophie

## Design Rules

- Let photographs breathe.
- Use generous white space.
- Keep interface elements minimal.
- Preserve original words exactly.
- Never invent private events.
