The Yacht

First of the Class 44M

Juno's 7 is the first Benetti Class 44M, launched in Viareggio in November 2024 and delivered in 2025. She is part of Benetti's fibreglass Class range and was designed by Cassetta Yacht Designers.

Deck plan summary

How the Class 44M is arranged

Public articles describe Juno's 7 as a four-deck yacht. This simplified plan is for journal context rather than a technical drawing.

Sun Deck
Large open-air deck of roughly 102 m², with space for sunbathing, shaded relaxation and panoramic views.
Upper Deck
Helm station, captain's cabin and a generous indoor-outdoor living area extending aft. Some descriptions mention optional VIP accommodation.
Main Deck
Owner's suite, galley, dayhead and spacious lounge with large windows and direct views towards the water.
Lower Deck
Guest cabins and crew area, with the beach club at water level and garage arranged to port.
