The Yacht

First of the Class 44M

Juno's 7 is the first Benetti Class 44M, launched in Viareggio in November 2024 and delivered in 2025. She is part of Benetti's fibreglass Class range and was designed by Cassetta Yacht Designers, who worked on both exterior and interior styling.
