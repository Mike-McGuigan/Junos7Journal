The idea

A working yacht is a small floating team

Behind every calm anchorage, tidy deck, guest trip ashore and smooth arrival is a crew working in the background. This page explains the roles in broad terms rather than naming private crew members.

Departments

Roles aboard

Captain

Overall command of the yacht, safety, navigation, crew leadership and guest operations.

Deck Crew

Exterior maintenance, mooring, tenders, toys, washdowns and guest activities on the water. Cameron’s updates often show this side of life aboard.

Interior Crew

Housekeeping, cabins, laundry, service, guest presentation and the details that make life aboard feel effortless. Sophie’s perspective helps capture this world in the journal.

Engineering

Engines, generators, air conditioning, water systems, hydraulics, electrics and the invisible systems that keep the yacht moving.

Chef

Provisioning and preparing meals around guest preferences, passages, anchorages and shore access.

Guests and activities

Tenders, jet skis, swim platforms and water toys all need preparation, watchfulness and safe handling from the crew.
